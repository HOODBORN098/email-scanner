Security Protocol: Heuristic Analysis

"The sentinel never sleeps, looking not for known patterns, but for the intent of malice."

01 Signal Decryption

Our engine decrypts incoming signals at the edge layer. This ensures that even obfuscated scripts or polymorphic payloads are rendered visible for behavioral analysis before they touch the primary internal network.

02 Behavioral Sandboxing

Each suspicious attachment is executed in a volatile, isolated micro-VM. Clinical Sentinel monitors system-level changes, registry modifications, and outbound network attempts to classify the payload's intent.

warning Critical Alert Configuration

Failure to properly configure the 'Auto-Isolation' flag can lead to delayed responses during massive phishing campaigns. Ensure your API keys have the 'WRITE_QUARANTINE' scope enabled.
